## Cycle 01 — Bedrock · 2026-07-13 · NEW

**The read** — 35F, ~61kg, 3+ yrs trained, general fitness; priority = glute hypertrophy,
then fat loss + injury prevention. Recovery: 7–8h sleep at 6/10 quality, stress 5/10,
life-load HEAVY — but she already trains 5+ d/wk, so frequency isn't novel; the lever is
per-day load distribution (one peak day, run splitting the two lower days), not day count.
Volume ceiling set moderate: glutes 14–16 hard sets/wk (developing), everything else
maintenance-plus. No baseline lifts given → week 1 is deliberate calibration (RPE ~6–7,
trust the bar over the résumé). L4-L5 disc history, currently pain-free: no heavy axial
squatting (her standing medical instruction), no supine leg-raise core (her known trigger —
dead bugs excluded too, same mechanism), free hinge deferred to C2 per chronic-back
principle; C1 hinge = hip thrust anchor + light cable pull-through as the groove drill that
earns C2's RDL. Aerobic red flag: 177 avg / 186 peak HR at ~7:00/km (Jul 4 run) — she reads
the fast HR ramp as "natural"; coach reads it as an underdeveloped aerobic base. Run day is
zone-2 at HR ≤145 with walk-breaks; HR-at-fixed-pace becomes the tracked marker across the
arc. Equipment confirmed: barbell+plates, leg press, cable stack, full DB rack (condo gym).

**Decisions** —
PRIMARY LIFT SELECTIONS:
  Glutes/hip extension → Barbell Hip Thrust — top glute SFR, zero axial compression
  Unilateral lower     → Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squat — high glute stimulus, minimal spinal load
  Squat pattern        → Machine Leg Press (light-moderate; heavy squatting contraindicated)
  Upper pull           → Chest-Supported Dumbbell Row — trunk-supported per principle
  Upper push           → Seated Dumbbell Overhead Press — backrest = spinal support
Checkpoint: Amir approved analysis + selections as-is ("Build it"). Equipment fork resolved
by Amir (full condo gym). Injury fork resolved by Amir pre-design: pain-free, told not to
squat heavy, cannot do supine leg-raise core, knows to stop on pain. 5 days confirmed
despite heavy life-load — managed via undulation (peak/mod/low/mod-high/low), not refusal.
Structure: 2 lower + 1 upper + 1 full-body + 1 zone-2 run; no supersets (first cycle).
Fallbacks: Hip Thrust → Dumbbell Hip Thrust · Leg Press → Heels-Elevated Goblet Squat ·
BSS → Dumbbell Reverse Lunge · CS Row → Seated Cable Row · Seated OHP → Machine Shoulder Press.

**Revision (2026-07-08, pre-launch, Amir):** first draft under-filled the time budget for a
3+ yr lifter already training 5×/wk — Day 1/2/4 each landed at only 4 working exercises
against a ~60-min session cap that had 20–25 min of unused headroom, and Day 3's pre-run prep
was a bare 2-item list while every other day had a proper 3-item circuit. Fixed: added Cable
Hip Abduction to Day 1 (glute medius — rounds out the glute goal beyond hip thrust/kickback,
low-cost isolation, keeps Day 1 spine-safe), added Box Step-Up to Day 4 (second unilateral
glute/quad driver alongside BSS, vertical loading pattern — no axial spine load, high SFR for
the glute goal), and rebuilt Day 3's prep into a proper Run Prep Circuit (Leg Swings + Ankle
Dorsiflexion Rocks + Forward Skip) matching the other days' warm-up depth. Days 1/2/4 now all
sit at 5 working exercises, Day 5 at 6 — undulation shape (peak/mod/low/mod-high/low) unchanged,
still fits the time cap, still zero new axial spinal load. No change to the locked primary
selections or the spine-safety rules.
