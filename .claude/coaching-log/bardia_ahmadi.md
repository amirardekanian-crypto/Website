## Cycle 01 — Bedrock · 2026-07-11 · NEW

**The read** — 28M, 76 kg, lifelong lifter, padel player 5 months post mid-competition injury. Right knee: low-grade partial ACL tear + suspected peripheral posterior medial meniscus tear, no effusion — needs progressive loading, not protection. Right hip: superior labral tear on bilateral acetabular dysplasia (right-predominant) — structural, permanent; dynamic stabilizers must do the joint's missing passive work forever. Cleared to train with limits; physio glute/core phase done; playing padel 2–3×/wk (flexible days) with little pain; deep hip flexion under axial load is the pain trigger. Recovery capacity good (sleep 7/10, stress 5/10, nutrition solid, whey + creatine) — the ceiling is joint tolerance + court load, not recovery. Structure: Upper/Lower ×2 over full-body ×4 for clean pain-attribution and true low days; undulated peak (Lower A hinge) / moderate (Upper A, Lower B) / low (Upper B). No end-range hip stretching (dysplastic laxity); hip-thrust lockout PPT-gated; unilateral work right-side-first as the tolerance gauge; no loaded rotation yet (court supplies it; C2 introduces it controlled).

**Decisions** — PRIMARY LIFT SELECTIONS: hinge → Barbell RDL (hip-safe zone, transfer, e1RM-trackable) · hip extension → Barbell Hip Thrust (glute max without flexion depth) · knee-dominant → Dumbbell Split Squat (quad load, shallow stance-hip flexion, exposes R/L gap; → Bulgarian in C2) · upper pull → Chest-Supported Dumbbell Row · upper push → Barbell Bench Press (volume held below pull). Forks settled by Amir at checkpoint: **squat audit = bodyweight only, in warm-ups** (Squat to Stand 2×/wk; no loaded squat probe in C1 — loaded goblet earliest C2 if audit clean); **padel = 2–3 flexible sessions** → flexible week ordering with hard rules (Lower A never before padel; lower days never adjacent). Nordics deliberately held for C2; Machine Lying Leg Curl carries hamstring volume meanwhile. **Session structure: straight sets, not supersets**, for all accessory work this cycle (Leg Curl/Step-Down, Landmine Press/Face Pull, Leg Extension/Hip Abduction) — Amir's call: week 1 is already calibrating unknown working weights on every lift, and bouncing between two stations to find two unknown loads at once adds friction exactly when we want that week simple. Revisit supersetting from C2 once baselines are established.

**Progression levers** — RDL: ride reps 6→8, then +5 kg; trigger = 8 @ RPE ≤7; any back rounding or hip niggle → back to 6 @ prior load. Hip Thrust: +5–10 kg at 10 clean reps with held PPT; ANY anterior-hip pinch at lockout → immediate range cap, log it. Split Squat: reps 8→10/side then +2 kg/hand; right side gates both — asymmetry >2 reps means hold the left back. Bench: +2.5 kg at 8 @ RPE ≤7. Row: +2 kg at 10 clean. No scheduled deload in the 5 weeks — coach-driven from readiness + ACWR with the court load in view; if week 4 combined load reads high, week 5 becomes a back-off.

**e1RM** — none yet; baselines emerge from weeks 1–2 logs (week 1 runs RPE −1 as calibration).
