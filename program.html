How to use this app — read once, refer back anytime
🧭
Getting around

Open the app from your home screen. The homepage shows your current cycle and progress.

Tap Current Program to start training, or Notes to read the personal notes I write you each cycle. Inside Current Program, the tabs at the top (D1, D2, D3…) switch between training days.

💪
RPE & Tempo

RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) = how hard a set felt, on a scale of 1–10. RPE 7 means you finished with about 3 reps left in the tank. RPE 10 means you couldn't have done another rep.

Tempo = the speed of each rep, written as 4 numbers like 3-1-1-0. Example for a squat: 3 sec down, 1 sec pause at the bottom, 1 sec up, 0 sec pause at the top.

✏️
Logging your sets

Tap an exercise to expand it. Add the weight you used, your RPE for the set, and any quick note (felt heavy, knee tight, etc.).

Tap the circle to mark the set done. Everything saves automatically — if you close the app and come back, your inputs are still there.

✅
Finish Workout → Send Data to CoachImportant

When every exercise of a session is checked off, a Finish Workout card appears at the bottom. Add your overall session RPE and a session note, then tap Send Data to Coach.

Don't skip this step. It's how I see your numbers and feedback for the week. Without it I can't adjust your program properly.

📅
What to report & how often

Every session → use the Send Data to Coach button (above).

Every Sunday → quick WhatsApp with how the week felt: sleep, stress, niggles, tennis load.

Anytime → pain that lasts more than 48 hours, missed sessions, or anything that feels off.

📲
Install the app on your home screen

iPhone: open this page in Safari, tap the Share button, then "Add to Home Screen".

Android: open in Chrome, tap the menu (⋮), then "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen".

Once installed it opens like a normal app — full-screen, no browser bar.

🛠️
Built-in tools
  • Rest timer — tap the timer icon on an exercise to start a countdown between sets.
  • Exercise videos — most exercises have a video link. Tap it if you're unsure of form.
  • Dark mode — tap the 🌙 button on the program screen for low-light gym sessions.
💬
Questions or problems

Message me on WhatsApp anytime — form check videos, equipment swaps ("my gym doesn't have this machine, what should I do instead?"), or anything you're unsure about. Don't sit on something for days; quick check-ins beat long waits.
