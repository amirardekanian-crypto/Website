⚠ Two hard Farsi rules — never break these

1 · Never apply letter-spacing
It breaks Persian cursive joining — letters disconnect. Every .fa slide forces letter-spacing: 0.
2 · No UPPERCASE · Persian numerals
Uppercase is meaningless in Farsi. Use Persian digits — ۰۱ / ۰۸, not 01 / 08.
